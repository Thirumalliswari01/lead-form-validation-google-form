Lead Management Workflow

Description of the Solution

The proposed workflow for TechNova Solutions addresses lead management by automating key processes, ensuring efficiency and scalability. This solution includes the following:

1. Basic Lead Capture and Scoring:

  • A form is used to collect essential lead details, including company size, budget, industry, urgency, and comments.
  • A scoring system is designed to prioritize leads based on criteria like company size, budget, and urgency.
  • Automation processes calculate scores, categorize leads, and take appropriate actions.

2. Addressing Edge Cases:

  • Incomplete data is routed for review and follow-up.
  • High-value leads are flagged for immediate attention.
  • Time zones are accommodated for scheduling follow-ups.

3. Scaling and Advanced Implementation:

  • Sales reps are assigned leads evenly using a dedicated system.
  • Keywords are extracted from the "Comments" field to categorize leads.
  • A follow-up system ensures timely interactions with leads.
